WEFARM

WEFARM is vertical cultivator that maximize growth with various color of light from UV to IR.

Easy access with phone that allow to control the condition of cultivator and watch the plant.

how to use

1. Order plant from app.
2. Get seedling package.
3. Put seedling in pot.
4. Place pot in WEFARM.
5. Fill water tank.
6. Plant growing.
7. Take out oit and harvest.

Different plants need different light color to maximize growth.

Start with 1:1 cardboard mockups to understand context better. This approach helps to increase usability to various heights.

making of




WEFARM

In home, easy to kill plant due to unstable and not friendly environment.

WEFARM is a cultivator that help cultivation easy to use in home. WEFARM can make various light color that helps make friendly environment to the plant. In addtion, WE FARM provide refular light pattern and water supply in closed space. With accessing cultivator with app, user can take care of house plant all the time.

WEFRM is able to do the design from the user’s point of view by making various ideas quickly into real size mock-ups. Mostly use carboard and wire frame foam factor when making mock-ups
